Alex "Nova" Carter - Frontman & Guitarist

Born and raised in Camden, Alex "Nova" Carter grew up surrounded by the gritty pulse of London's underground rock and punk scenes. His parents were artists who exposed him to a diverse range of music, from Bowie and Led Zeppelin to the more experimental sounds of the 80s. As a teenager, Nova found an affinity for metal, captivated by its raw energy and emotional depth. After a few stints in different local bands, he developed a unique playing style that blends intricate riffs with a spacey atmosphere, reflective of his fascination with astronomy and science fiction. Nova's lyrics often tackle existential questions about humanity's place in the universe and the potential for life beyond Earth, mirroring his intense curiosity and reflective nature. Known for his haunting vocals and intense presence, he became the gravitational force that holds SolarSparks together, using music as a way to explore both inner and outer worlds.

Mia "Blaze" Torres - Bassist

Originally from Manchester, Mia "Blaze" Torres moved to London in her early twenties, drawn by its thriving music scene. Blaze picked up the bass guitar at age 12 and quickly developed a playing style that fuses heavy metal's weight with a punk rock edge, giving her a powerful, thunderous sound. Her rebellious spirit and fierce independence made her an integral part of SolarSparks' formation; she connected immediately with Nova, sharing his passion for music that could transcend earthly limitations. Blaze also brings a political edge to the band, channeling her experiences of growing up in a working-class neighborhood into lyrics and riffs that challenge authority and corporate greed. Outside of music, she's deeply involved in London's DIY punk scene, often organizing benefit shows for social causes, further rooting SolarSparks in a sense of community and resistance.

Jake "The Pulse" Rivers - Drummer

Jake Rivers, better known as "The Pulse," grew up in South London with a background in classical percussion, which later morphed into a passion for metal drumming. Jake is the band's rhythmic backbone, known for his stamina and dynamic playing style, which can shift from the slow doom of stoner metal to rapid-fire thrash in a heartbeat. Before joining SolarSparks, he played with various hardcore bands, developing a reputation as one of the most versatile drummers on the local scene. His skill with polyrhythms and unconventional time signatures added a unique layer to SolarSparks' sound, giving their music a pulsating, unpredictable quality. Jake is fascinated by ancient civilizations, especially their myths and rhythms, which often inspire the intricate drum patterns in SolarSparks' songs. Behind the kit, he's a force of nature, bringing a primal energy to their live performances.

Ellie "Neon" Malik - Synthesizer & Sound Engineer

Ellie "Neon" Malik was born in Brixton to a family of scientists, but she found her passion in electronic music instead of the laboratory. As a child, she would sneak into her father's study to experiment with synthesizers and sound software, eventually developing a sound that blends darkwave, industrial, and ambient textures. Her stage name, “Neon,” reflects both her love for vivid, futuristic soundscapes and her fascination with light's role in the universe. Neon's contribution to SolarSparks is her ethereal synth lines and cosmic sound effects, which transport listeners to otherworldly realms. Known for her quiet, introspective nature, Neon spends much of her time programming visual effects and projections for the band's live shows, enhancing the immersive experience. Her scientific background also plays a role in her approach to music, treating each song as an experiment in sound, often pushing the boundaries of traditional metal with her atmospheric touch.
